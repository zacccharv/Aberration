Controls
--

Game Controls

  • The arrow keys on your keyboard or WASD.
  • Press the direction on your keyboard that matches the arrow closest to the center when the center diamond is green.
Press left once.
 Press up twice.
 Press & hold right.

Menu Controls

  • Esc pauses the game, it also goes back a page in the menu.
  • Enter confirms your selection in menus.
  • Left and right control the volume sliders.

Description
--

A game inspired somewhat by "Dance Dance Revolution" and other rhythm games, except you use your fingers instead of your feet. Made for Acerola's game jam in March 2024. The prompt was aberration, which I defined as a departure from what is normal. So arrows on the screen don't always match the direction they come from.
